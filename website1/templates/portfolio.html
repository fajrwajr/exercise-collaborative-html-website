Starscream

Commonly referred to as second command, Starscream wants nothing more than to be leader of the Decepticons but he is never taken seriously as he always retreats when the battle becomes difficult.

Appearance: Transformers 1987

Ratchet

Although he's a healer, Ratchet often finds himself on the front lines of combat and, on occasion, the last hope the Autobots have. These pressures are sometimes too much for him to handle, but Ratchet ultimately never gives up.

Chromia

Chromia is a seasoned, tough-as-nails infiltrator. She can also be fiercely protective, which makes her an exceptional bodyguard. Her rough edges often find her a kinship with other security officers such as Ironhide.

Arcee

Arcee is a ruthless and deadly warrior who's an expert in hand-to-hand combat and one of the best sharpshooters on record. The reason for her ferocity is how much she cares for her fellow Autobots and especially the humans they protect.
